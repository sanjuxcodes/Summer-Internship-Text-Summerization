

Arjun lived in a coastal town where storms were common during the rainy season. He was a smart and responsible boy who always listened carefully to weather reports. One evening, the television news announced that a powerful cyclone was approaching the area. The government advised people to stay indoors and remain prepared.

Many villagers ignored the warning because the weather still appeared calm. However, Arjun noticed dark clouds gathering in the distance and strong winds beginning to blow. He felt that the storm could become dangerous very quickly. He immediately informed his parents about the situation.

Together, the family started preparing their house. They closed all windows tightly and stored drinking water, candles, and food supplies. Arjun also helped his elderly neighbor carry important belongings indoors. The neighbor appreciated his kindness and concern.

As night approached, the storm became extremely powerful. Heavy rain poured continuously, and loud thunder shook the houses. Electricity in the town stopped working, leaving the streets completely dark. Many people became frightened because trees were falling and roads were flooding rapidly.

Inside the house, Arjun remained calm. He comforted his younger sister and kept listening to emergency updates on a battery-powered radio. He reminded everyone not to go outside until the authorities declared the area safe. His maturity surprised his parents.

The cyclone finally weakened the next morning. Although several houses were damaged, Arjun’s family remained safe because they had prepared in advance. Rescue workers praised citizens who followed safety instructions responsibly. Arjun’s neighbor told everyone how the boy had helped during the emergency.

The incident taught the villagers the importance of listening to warnings and preparing for natural disasters. Arjun understood that awareness and quick action can save lives. His bravery and responsibility became an example for others in the community.
