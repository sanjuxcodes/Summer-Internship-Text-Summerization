
Riya was a hardworking college student who lived in a small town. Every morning, she traveled by bus to attend her classes in the city. One rainy afternoon, after finishing her exams, she boarded a crowded bus to return home. The bus was full of passengers carrying umbrellas and bags. Riya sat near the window and looked outside at the busy streets.

After reaching home, she realized that her wallet was missing. The wallet contained some money, her identity card, and an important library card. She became very worried because she needed those documents the next day. She searched her bag several times but could not find the wallet anywhere.

Riya immediately remembered the crowded bus journey. She thought the wallet might have fallen from her bag while she was trying to take out her phone. Her mother advised her to stay calm and think carefully. Riya decided to visit the bus depot the next morning.

The following day, she reached the depot early. She spoke politely with the bus manager and explained the situation. The manager checked the lost-and-found section but initially found nothing. Riya felt disappointed. However, one of the bus conductors overheard the conversation and remembered seeing a wallet on a seat after the last trip.

The conductor brought the wallet and asked Riya to identify it. She quickly recognized the small blue wallet with a silver chain. Everything inside it was safe. Riya thanked the conductor sincerely for his honesty and kindness. She felt relieved and happy.

Before leaving, the conductor advised her to keep important belongings carefully while traveling. Riya learned an important lesson that day. From then on, she always checked her belongings before getting off any bus. She also understood that honest people still exist in society. The experience made her more responsible and careful in daily life.
