

In a small village surrounded by green fields, there lived a farmer named Mohan. He worked very hard every day to support his family. Mohan owned a small piece of land where he grew rice and vegetables. Although his income was not very high, he remained honest and content with his life.

Every morning before sunrise, Mohan went to the fields with his tools and oxen. He watered the crops, removed weeds, and checked the condition of the soil. During summer, the heat was intense, but he never complained. He believed that hard work would always bring success.

One year, the village faced a serious drought. Rainfall was very low, and the crops started drying up. Many farmers became hopeless because they feared losing their harvest. Mohan was also worried, but he did not give up. He started using water carefully and created small channels to distribute water evenly across the fields.

His children also helped him after school. They carried water buckets and supported their father in every possible way. Mohan’s wife encouraged the family to remain positive during difficult times. Their unity gave them strength and courage.

After several weeks, dark clouds finally appeared in the sky. Heavy rain began to fall across the village. The farmers celebrated happily because their crops were saved. Mohan’s field survived better than many others because of his careful planning and hard work.

At harvest time, Mohan produced a good amount of crops. The village head praised him for his determination and wisdom. Other farmers visited him to learn his farming methods. Mohan shared his ideas freely because he believed knowledge should help everyone.

The story of Mohan teaches that patience, teamwork, and dedication can overcome difficulties. Even during hard times, a person should never lose hope. Success often comes to those who continue working with determination and confidence.
